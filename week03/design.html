Meet Peter Ella Bisong, a Student from Nigeria

Peter Ella Bisong grew up in Kaduna, Kaduna State in northwestern Nigeria, where mornings often begin with golden sunlight spreading across the savanna and rocky hills. He moved to Abuja years back and he is to studying Software Development, and he brings a sharp eye for color and negative space to every project he touches.

When he isn’t in the studio, Peter Ella Bisong volunteers with a local conservation group that maps hiking trails for accessibility, work that quietly shapes how he thinks about clear, legible design for everyone.

Quick Facts

  • Hometown: Cross River, Nigeria
  • Studying: Graphic Design
  • Languages: Hausa, Nigerian Pidgin English
  • Favorite tool: Grid layouts
  • Hobby: Writing
